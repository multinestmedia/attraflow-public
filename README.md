# AttraFlow

> **Track the Flow of Your Attraction**

A privacy-first web application for gaining personal insight by tracking and visualizing your experiences of attraction, gender identity feelings, and personal preferences over time.

## About AttraFlow

AttraFlow is designed for individuals exploring their sexual identity and attraction patterns. Whether you're questioning, fluid, or simply curious about your experiences, AttraFlow provides a safe, private space to record and reflect on your feelings.

### Key Features

- **Daily Check-ins**: Record your attraction levels, gender feelings, and preferences
- **Visual Analytics**: See patterns and trends in your data over time
- **Complete Privacy**: All data stays on your device - no accounts, no servers, no tracking
- **Fluid-Friendly**: Designed for identities and attractions that change over time
- **Export Your Data**: Full control to export or delete your information anytime

---

## Privacy First

**Your data never leaves your device.** AttraFlow stores everything locally in your browser using localStorage. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

**Important Notes**

- **Data Persistence**: Your data persists only in the browser/device where you enter it
- **No Sync**: Data is not synced across devices
- **Browser Data**: Clearing browser data will delete all your entries
- **Backup**: Export your data regularly as a backup

## Getting Started

### Try It Now

Visit [AttraFlow.com](attraflow.com) or [AttraFlow.app](AttraFlow.app) to start using the application immediately - no sign-up required!

### What You Can Track

- **Attraction Levels**: Rate your attraction to your gender identity and others
- **Gender Feelings**: Track how you feel on the gender spectrum
- **Position/Role Preferences**: Note your preferences (optional)
- **Personal Notes**: Add context and reflections

### First Time Setup

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Set your preferred theme (light/dark mode)
3. Start your first daily submission
4. Return anytime to add new entries or view your data

## How It Works

1. **Submit**: Record your daily feelings and experiences
2. **Visualize**: View your data in charts and tables
3. **Reflect**: Identify patterns and gain insights
4. **Export**: Download your data anytime

All processing happens in your browser - no data ever leaves your device.

---

## Technical Details

- **Frontend**: React 19 + TypeScript + Vite
- **UI Framework**: Tailwind CSS v4 + Shadcn/ui
- **Data Storage**: TinyBase (local-first, localStorage-backed)
- **Routing**: React Router
- **Forms**: React Hook Form
