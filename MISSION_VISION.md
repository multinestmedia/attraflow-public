# Mission and Vision Statements
## Mission
Our mission is to empower individuals on their journey of self-discovery by providing an intuitive platform for tracking, visualizing, and reflecting on their experiences of attraction. Through seamless daily check-ins, insightful data visualizations, and interactive tools, we aim to promote self-awareness, celebrate fluidity, and deepen understanding of diverse sexual identities.

## Vision
To foster a more self-aware and inclusive world where individuals can confidently explore, understand, and embrace the natural rhythms of their sexual identity and attraction.
