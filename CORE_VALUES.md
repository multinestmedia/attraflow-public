# Core Values

## Inclusivity
We embrace and celebrate the full spectrum of consenting sexual identities and attractions, creating a space that supports all users on their journey of self-discovery and understanding.

## Empowerment
We equip users with intuitive tools to explore and visualize their experiences of attraction, fostering self-awareness and confidence in their unique identities.

## Privacy and Security
We prioritize user privacy by ensuring that personal data is secure, transparent in its use, and controlled by the user, reinforcing trust and confidentiality.

## Fluidity
We honor the dynamic nature of attraction and identity, recognizing that experiences can ebb and flow over time. Our app is designed to adapt to and celebrate these changes.

## User-Centric Design
We are committed to delivering an intuitive, accessible, and aesthetically pleasing experience, focusing on simplicity and meaningful insights tailored to user needs.

## Data Integrity and Ethics
We handle user data responsibly, adhering to ethical standards that prioritize transparency, user consent, and anonymization when appropriate.

## Community and Support
We aim to create a supportive environment by offering resources, insights, and connections that enhance the user's journey toward self-acceptance and understanding.
